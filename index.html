01
What We Do
Real-Time Weather App

Weather Tracker is a web-based app that gives you instant access to current temperature, conditions, and a full 24-hour hourly forecast for any city — on desktop or mobile.

02
The Problem
Fragmented, Unreliable Forecasts

Users lack access to a single, accurate, real-time weather source. Without reliable forecasts, daily planning for commuters, students, farmers, and professionals becomes guesswork.

03
The Impact
Poor Decisions & Disrupted Plans

Inconsistent weather data leads to missed travel plans, disrupted outdoor activities, and safety risks. People deserve a consistent, trustworthy source they can rely on every day.

04
Our Solution
One Dashboard. All the Data.

Powered by the OpenWeather API, Weather Tracker fetches live data and presents current conditions plus a 24-hour hourly forecast in a clean, mobile-friendly interface — no clutter, no confusion.
