01
What We Do
All-in-One Farm Platform

SmartFarm Assist is a web-friendly platform delivering real-time weather, local crop market prices, expert farm management tips, and crop-specific seasonal guides — all with zero registration.

02
The Problem
Farmers Lack Reliable Data

Smallholder farmers across Rwanda and Africa struggle to access accurate weather forecasts, fair market prices, and reliable crop guidance — leading to poor decisions and reduced income.

03
The Impact
Food Security at Risk

Without reliable information, productivity drops and crop losses increase. This threatens livelihoods of millions of farming families and undermines food security across the continent.

04
Our Solution
Instant. Offline. Private.

Open the app and instantly see what matters. Data is cached locally for offline use in areas with poor connectivity. No accounts, no passwords, no personal data collected. Just useful information, immediately.
